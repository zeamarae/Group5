Puto Bumbong

Puto bumbong is a cherished Filipino delicacy made of purple-hued glutinous rice steamed in bamboo tubes, traditionally enjoyed during the Christmas season. Served with butter, grated coconut, and muscovado sugar, its unique flavor and festive charm make it a Noche Buena favorite.
